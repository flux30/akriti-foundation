Trip to Maher Ashram, Pune

Akriti Foundation recently organized an enriching and memorable trip to Maher Ashram in Pune. Maher, a non-governmental organization, is known for its outstanding work in providing shelter, care, and support to destitute women and children.

Our visit aimed to connect the children we support with the larger community and expose them to different aspects of life. The trip included interactive sessions, educational activities, and engaging interactions with the residents of Maher Ashram.

Maher Ashram, nestled in the serene surroundings of Pune, serves as a haven for those in need. The children from Akriti Foundation had the opportunity to learn about the Ashram's mission, experience a sense of community, and participate in various recreational activities.
